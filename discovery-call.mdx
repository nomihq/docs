# 🔍 How to Make Great Discovery Calls

> Discovery calls are 70% of the sale.  
> Here’s how to turn them into real pipeline — fast.  
>  
> ⏱️ **Reading time: 1 min 30**  
> Made by [heynomi.com](https://heynomi.com)


## 🧠 Ask 11–14 Questions — Not 20

Sweet spot: 11–14 questions  
10? Too shallow. >15? Feels like an interrogation  
🎯 Be curious, not robotic  
> Source: Gong – Essential Discovery Call Techniques




## 🚫 Ditch Lazy Openers

❌ “Tell me about your business?”  
✅ “What’s your current process for X — where’s it breaking?”  
> _Source: Gong – The Discovery Question Killing Your Credibility_



## 🎯 Focus on 3–4 Key Pains

Don’t skim 10 surface-level issues  
Go deep on a few critical ones  
✅ “What are the top 3 challenges you're facing with X?”  
> _Source: Gong – Essential Discovery Call Techniques_



## ⏳ Ask About Urgency + Impact

✅ “What happens if nothing changes?”  
✅ “Is there a timeline in place?”  
> _Source: Calendly & Gong – Sales Stats and Discovery Call Questions_



## 💰 Qualify Budget & Decision-Maker – Later

✅ “What’s your budget range?”  
✅ “Who else will weigh in?”  
⚠️ Don’t start here. Build value first.  
> _Source: Gong – BANT Pitfalls_



## 🗣️ Keep It Conversational – Skip the Deck

No slides. Just talk, ask, listen, adjust  
> _Source: Gong – Sales Stats That Will Change How You Sell_



## ❓ Use Open-Ended Questions

✅ “Walk me through how you do X today”  
Target a **40:60 talk ratio** (you:them)  
> _Source: SPIN Selling & Gong_



## 🔁 Always Define Next Steps

✅ “What’s the logical next step from here?”  
Avoid vague endings — **book the follow-up**  
> _Source: Outreach – Discovery Process Strategies_



## 🎯 Personalize Everything

✅ “Saw you just launched in Europe — how’s that impacting ops?”  
Tailored = trust. Scripts = snooze  
> _Source: Outreach – Discovery Process Strategies_



## 🧑‍💼 Selling to Execs? Ask Fewer, Smarter Questions

~4 big-picture Qs — not 10 tactical ones  
✅ “What key metric are you focused on improving?”  
> _Source: Enterprise Sales / C-Suite Best Practice_



## 💡 Proven Frameworks to Use

- **SPIN**: Situation, Problem, Implication, Need-payoff  
- **BANT**: Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline  
- **MEDDIC**: Metrics, Economic Buyer, Decision Criteria, Decision Process, Identify Pain, Champion  

📌 Pick one. Stick to it. Customize.



## 📈 Real Results

Some YC sales teams hit:
- **73% Discovery → Demo conversion**
- **53.1% Close rate** (by qualifying hard early)

➡ More fit = more wins.  
➡ Less fluff = less churn.
